Data Deletion Policy

At Khabar Manager, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. This policy explains how you can delete your account and all associated data.

1. Types of Data Collected

  • Phone Number, Email, Address (from account settings)
  • Meal records and logs
  • Expense and payment details
  • User profile information
  • App usage data for analytics and performance

2. Deleting Your Account

Users can delete their account directly from the Account Settings page. Steps include:

  • Go to Account Settings.
  • Tap the "Delete Account" option.
  • Confirm your decision to delete.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data including phone number, email, address, meal records, expenses, and app usage data will be permanently deleted from our servers and devices.

3. Data Recovery

After deletion, data cannot be recovered. Please ensure you back up any important information before deleting your account.

4. Offline & Online Data

Whether your data is stored locally on your device or synced online, deleting your account will remove it from all locations associated with your account.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or need assistance with account deletion, please contact our support team at support@khabarmanager.com.

By using Khabar Manager, you agree to this Data Deletion Policy.
